My Favorite Food is Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and a choice of protein like chicken, lamb, or vegetables. Layers of marinated meat or vegetables are slow-cooked with rice and saffron-infused milk, resulting in a rich, multi-layered, and mouthwatering culinary experience. It's a beloved and diverse dish with regional variations across India and other parts of South Asia.
